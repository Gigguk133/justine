About me

Hi! I’m Justine Monton, a 3rd-year Information Technology student. I’m still learning the ropes of web development, but I’m getting better every day. Sometimes it feels like coding is a game—sometimes you win, sometimes you lose, and sometimes you just have to restart everything. I’m working on both front-end and back-end development, and I love figuring out how things work. I’ve learned that coding, like gaming, can be tough, but it’s always worth it when you solve the problem. “Why does my code break right when I’m about to finish?” – the mystery of coding! When I’m not coding, I’m probably playing online games. They help me relax and have fun, just like solving a tough coding problem. “In coding and gaming, the best moments come when you finally figure things out!”
